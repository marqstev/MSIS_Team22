About Us

Oconee County Fire Rescue is mostly a volunteer organization in Onconee County, GA. Its fire fighters and rescue personnel have taken on many obstacles and objectives to improve the community with personal engagement. Oconee County Fire Rescue will continue to serve the right way!

Mission

The mission of Oconee County Fire Rescue is to save lives and property, to strive to provide the most optimal emergency services for its citizens, and to advocate fire safety to make Oconee County a better, safer place to live.
